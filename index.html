Our Story

Where Dreams Become Memories

◆

"From intimate celebrations to grand events, we design experiences that become lifelong memories."

Chinmoy Events & Wedding Planner was born from a passion for creating extraordinary moments. Based in Bankura, West Bengal, we have grown into the region's most trusted event house — weaving traditional richness with modern elegance. Our team handles every detail from conception to the final bow, ensuring each event reflects the unique story of our clients.

With the capacity to execute events of all scales and coverage across Bankura and neighbouring districts, we bring world-class execution to every celebration.
